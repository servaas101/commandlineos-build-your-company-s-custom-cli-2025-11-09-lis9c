About CommandlineOS

CommandlineOS was founded in 2021 by a team of experienced DevOps engineers and software architects who recognized a critical gap in the development tools market. After spending years managing complex CLI deployments across multiple organizations, they realized that companies were spending enormous amounts of time and money building custom command-line interfaces from scratch. This insight sparked the creation of CommandlineOS — a platform designed to democratize CLI development and make powerful command-line tools accessible to teams of all sizes. What started as a solution for internal use quickly evolved into a comprehensive platform used by hundreds of companies worldwide.

Today, CommandlineOS is committed to empowering development teams with tools that simplify complexity, reduce operational costs, and accelerate time-to-value. We believe that every company deserves access to enterprise-grade CLI infrastructure without the enterprise-grade price tag. Our mission is to make CLI development faster, easier, and more accessible than ever before. We're driven by our core values of innovation, simplicity, and customer success — and we're dedicated to continuously improving our platform based on the feedback and needs of our growing community of users.
